# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in ParseOtter respectful, inclusive, and productive.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on technical decisions and user impact.
- Assume good intent while still asking for clarity.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project.
- Respect privacy and do not post private data, credentials, or user documents.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Posting secrets, exploit details, or vulnerable deployment information in public issues.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block participants who violate this code of conduct.

For conduct concerns, contact the repository maintainers through the published
GitHub organization or maintainer contact path. Do not post private conduct
reports, credentials, exploit details, or user documents in public issues.
